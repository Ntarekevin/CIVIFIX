# CIVIFIX Authority & Citizen Guide

## For Citizens
1. **Access**: Go to `civifix.rw` on your phone or computer.
2. **Submit**: Tap "Report Issue". 
3. **Anonymity**: Provide category, description, location, and photos. Do NOT include your own name or contact details. The system scrubs GPS data from photos.
4. **Offline**: If you lose internet, your report is saved locally and will auto-submit when you get a connection.
5. **Track**: Upon submission, copy the long Tracking Token. Use it on the "Track Report" page to check if authorities are investigating your issue.

## For Authorities
1. **Access Portal**: Go to `civifix.rw/admin`
2. **Login**: Enter your provided username and password. 
3. **Dashboard**: 
   - You will see a map with **precise** coordinates of incident reports.
   - You will receive real-time pop-up alerts when new critical reports are filed.
4. **Actioning Cases**:
   - In the table below the map, you can review report details.
   - Use the **Assigned To** dropdown to allocate the case to a specific user (you or a team member).
   - Once work begins, change the **Status** to *In Progress*. Once fixed, mark as *Resolved*.
5. **Auditing**: Every status change, assignment, and view is recorded immutably in the system auditing log for oversight purposes. Do not share your login credentials.
